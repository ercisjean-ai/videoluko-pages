Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Information we use

VideoLuko uses only the information reasonably necessary to operate the app, such as account identifiers, authentication data, publishing settings, and content metadata related to scheduled or published videos.

TikTok account connection

When a user connects a TikTok account through OAuth or another authorized sign-in flow, VideoLuko may receive account and permission data required to enable publishing and account-related features. This information is used only within the scope of the granted access.

How data is used

Connected account data and publishing information are used for limited operational purposes, including authentication, session management, post preparation, publishing requests, and service support.

No sale of personal data

VideoLuko does not sell personal data and does not use connected account information for unrelated advertising purposes.

Data sharing and retention

Information is shared only when required to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or work with infrastructure providers acting on behalf of VideoLuko. Data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for operational, security, or legal purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please contact: contact@videoluko.example
